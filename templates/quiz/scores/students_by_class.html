    Column heading abbreviations:

    • Pre-test The student's score on the initial test.
    • AorA The student's score on the "Abuse or Accident?" activity.
    • Case 1 First case study
    • Case 2 Second case study
    • Case 3 Third case study
    • TA The student's score on the "Taking Action" reporting activity. If the student completed the reporting form, a button brings up a copy of the form as filled out by the student.
    • PT1 The student's initial score on the post-test, if available.
    • Passed Whether the post-test was passed with all questions correctly. If a date is available for this final successful test, it is given; otherwise (usually for students prior to 2010), 'ok' simply indicates that a student was able to complete all questions correctly.
    • Done To officially complete the training, the student needs to complete all the activities except TA, and successfully answer all the questions in the post-test.
