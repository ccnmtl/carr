Column heading abbreviations:

  • Pre-test The student's score on the initial test. The "»" buttons are links to the student's responses to the activities.
  • AorA The student's score on the "Abuse or Accident?" activity.
  • Case 1 First case study
  • Case 2 Second case study
  • Case 3 Third case study
  • TA The student's score on the "Taking Action" reporting activity. If the student completed the reporting form, a button brings up a copy of the form as filled out by the student.
  • PT1 The student's initial score on the post-test, if available. If you click on the "»" button you will be taken to the student's actual answers.
  • Passed Whether the post-test was passed with all questions correctly. If a date is available for this final successful test, it is given; otherwise (usually for students prior to 2010), 'ok' simply indicates that a student was able to complete all questions correctly. If you hold the mouse pointer over the abbreviated date, a complete date will appear.
  • Done To officially complete the training, the student needs to complete all the activities except TA, and successfully answer all the questions in the post-test.
